PHOENIX ECHO

Phoenix Echo carves out a niche in the synth-wave revival, blending cinematic 80s soundscapes with modern, introspective lyricism, earning comparisons to The Weeknd and M83. The debut single "Neon City Lights" achieved viral success, hitting the top 10 on the Spotify Global Viral 50 and receiving critical acclaim from Indie Music Digest. Currently in the studio, the artist is preparing for a multi-city European tour and the release of the highly anticipated sophomore EP, "Digital Ghost."
